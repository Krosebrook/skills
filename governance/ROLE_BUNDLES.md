# INTINC Role Bundles (Skills)

Role bundles are what you deploy. Each role gets only the skills they need.

## Bundle: L1 Helpdesk
Tier: 1 | Owner: Support Manager
Skills:
- L1 Helpdesk Intake
- Password Reset & Access Recovery
- Customer Empathy Response
- Service Desk Triage
- Dispatch: Field Services Scheduling (read-only)

## Bundle: Tier 2 / Escalations
Tier: 2 | Owner: Support Lead / EngOps
Skills:
- Tier 2 Escalation Resolver
- Field Tech Troubleshooter
- Onsite Work Summary
- Infrastructure Change Review
- Change Freeze Evaluator

## Bundle: SOC Analyst
Tier: 4 | Owner: SOC Lead
Skills:
- SOC Alert Correlation
- Security Alert Analysis
- Threat Hunt Planning
- Vulnerability Intake
- Security Incident Liaison

## Bundle: Account Manager / Client Success
Tier: 2 | Owner: Account Director
Skills:
- Client Executive Brief
- Client Environment Health Check
- Account Health Review
- Renewal Risk Assessment
- Stakeholder Translator

## Bundle: Finance
Tier: 3 | Owner: Finance Director
Skills:
- Payroll Exception Resolution

- Margin Impact Review

## Bundle: Compliance / Audit
